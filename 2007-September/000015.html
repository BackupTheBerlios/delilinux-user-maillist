 modem

If wvdial is installed on your System, try modem
detection with
$ su - root
# wvdialconf  /etc/wvdial.conf

If not you can try interrogating the /dev/ttyS0 to 3
ports with
whatever COMM tooles are provided in DeLi  Linux
